A confidential, impartial and free advice service for all Goldsmiths students with issues impacting their studies.

 

If you are experencing an academic issue that is impacting your studies at Goldsmiths, we are here to support you. 

Please read the guides below or get in touch to discuss your academic issue with one of our specialist advisers.

What is the Goldsmiths SU Advice Service?

The Goldsmiths SU Advice Service is a free, confidential and impartial service available for all students studying at Goldsmiths. Our friendly advisers will support you with any issues that are affecting your academic studies.
 

What can the Goldsmiths SU Advice Service help me with?

Academic Appeals including appealing final grades.

 

 

Appealing university decisions, including:
 

  • Academic misconduct
     
  • Termination of studies
     
  • 'Fitness to practice' decisions
     
  • Rejection of requests for reasonable adjustments for disabled students
     

Complaints against university decisions or services, including:
 

  • Dissatisfaction with the quality of a course
     
  • Dissatisfaction with the conduct of a staff member
     
  • Dissatisfaction with learning, teaching or student support services
     
  • Rejection of requests for reasonable adjustments for disabled students


If you are seeking support for any of the above, please follow the links below to find out more about the appeals and complaints processes or get in touch using this form.

 

Please note, the SU Advice Service is unable to provide financial and housing advice. Find out where you can gain non-academic support here.

 

  • Academic Support Gain advice and support appealing against University decisions, including Academic Misconduct and Final Grades.
  • Complaints Find out how to log a complaint about University decisions or services, including learning, teaching or student support services
  • Non-Academic Support Explore support from the College and external organisations for non-academic issues, including housing, finance, wellbeing and much more.
